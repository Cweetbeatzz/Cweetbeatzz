## Products @ Quecorex LLC

### Quecorex · `Healthcare SaaS`
Comprehensive hospital, clinic & pharmacy management platform. Covers EMR/EHR, pharmacy POS, DICOM/PACS radiology, laboratory, emergency department, telehealth, and full hospital operations — built for healthcare facilities globally.

`Angular` `Node.js` `PostgreSQL` `MongoDB` `Orthanc DICOM` `FHIR` `HIPAA` `Cloudflare R2`

---

### QueBucket · `AI Productivity SaaS`
Web-based tool for freelancers, consultants, and small businesses to generate professional invoices and client proposals in seconds using AI. Describe your service, client, and terms — AI produces polished, branded PDF/DOCX documents ready to send.

`AI` `PDF / DOCX` `Node.js` `Angular` `Stripe`

---

### QueBids · `Procurement Intelligence`
Global platform that aggregates government and corporate tender opportunities from hundreds of sources worldwide. Organized by region, country, sector, and budget — with personalized alerts so subscribers never miss a relevant contract opportunity.

`Data Aggregation` `Real-time Alerts` `Node.js` `Angular` `PostgreSQL`
